Clients geography

Artashat Cannery offers high quality and eco - friendly products made with special technology and in accordance with international standards. In addition to ready-to-eat products, Artashat Cannery also offers semi-processed products and fruit and vegetable concentrates which are exported and serve as raw material for major foreign producers.
The company is a major Armenian exporter. Only 13 % of the company's production is realized in the domestic market, while 87 % is exported. We currently deliver products to 23 countries:
